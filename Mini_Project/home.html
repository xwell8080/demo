Batac City

Home of Great Leaders

Historical Landmarks

Batac City is home to many historical landmarks, such as the Marcos Museum and Mausoleum, where you can learn about the life of former President Ferdinand Marcos.

Natural Beauty

With its lush landscapes and beautiful parks, Batac City offers visitors a chance to explore nature and experience its scenic beauty.

Delicious Local Cuisine

Batac is known for its flavorful empanadas, as well as its wide variety of local delicacies. Don't miss out on tasting the food that makes the city unique!
