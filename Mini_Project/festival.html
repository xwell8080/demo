Explore the Festival of Batac

Empanada Festival

The Empanada Festival is one of the most awaited events in Batac City. It celebrates the world-famous Batac Empanada, a delicious local snack made with a crispy shell filled with meat, vegetables, and egg. The festival features food stalls, street dancing, live music, and cultural performances. It is a feast for the senses!

Parade and Street Dancing

Experience the lively atmosphere of Batac's annual Parade and Street Dancing competition. The city streets are filled with vibrant colors as local performers in creative costumes dance to the beat of traditional music. It’s a celebration of Batac's rich culture and traditions, where everyone can join in the fun.

Cultural Night

Cultural Night is a spectacular event that brings together the artistic expressions of Batac's local artists. The event features traditional dances, music performances, and live art exhibitions, offering visitors a glimpse into the rich cultural heritage of Batac. It’s an evening of celebration and appreciation of local talent!
